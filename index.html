Welcome, Tenno

Welcome to Warframe Nexus — your gateway to the Origin System. This is more than just a fan site; it's a gathering place for Tenno around the world. Built by players, for players, Warframe Nexus offers news updates, beginner guides, community-created content, clan support, fan art, and lore discussions.

Whether you're a seasoned veteran mastering Kuva builds and Railjack missions or a new player just awakening to the Lotus' call, this space is crafted to help you evolve. Discover best-in-slot builds, share your FashionFrame styles, and contribute to one of the most welcoming and active game communities out there. We believe that Warframe isn’t just a game—it’s a universe, a story, and a bond between space ninjas that transcends platforms.

Our goal is to help every Tenno thrive. From alerts to events, from Devstreams to hotfixes, this site is your landing craft's console when you're away from the game. Stay informed, stay connected, and remember: the Tenno live.
